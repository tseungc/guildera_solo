Stock Phase

Tips and reminders

1. You can't buy new shares if you've already sold a share this round. ❌

2. If you sell your share in a guild, that guild's share price falls. 📉

3. You can't sell shares in an unfloated guild. ❌

4. Guilds are only floated if AT LEAST 4 SHARES have been bought. 🏰

5. If 8+ shares in 1 guild are owned by 3+ players, that guild's share price goes up twice. 📈

Raid Planning Phase

Tips and reminders

1. You can only hire 1 mercenary per round. 🧙

2. Make sure to equip your weapons. ⚔️

3. Decide what dungeon you want to raid before spending any money. 💭

4. If you raid a dungeon with a LOWER REWARD than last round, you will UNDERPERFORM, and your guild's stock price will fall. 📉
